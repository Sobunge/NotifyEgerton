Online Help

User Registration

To register to the site, contact the system administrator who will create the account and your username and password will be sent to your email.


User Login

To log-in, enter the username and password and click on the “Sign in” button on the log in page.

Creating communities

  1. Log in as System Administrator
  2. On the system administrator home page, click on the create community button.
    3. .
  3. On clicking the create community button, a community creation form will appear.
  4. Fill the form and click on the create button.
  5. After the community has been created, they will be displayed in the communities page as well as other communities previously created.

Creating groups

  1. Log in as System Administrator or the System users.
  2. On the system administrator homepage or the system user’s homepage, click on the create group button.
  3. On clicking the create group button, a form will appear
  4. Fill the form and click on the create button
  5. After the group has been created, they will be displayed in the groups page.
